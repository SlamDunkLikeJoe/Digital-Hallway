The brief these projects were based on was to create a "21st Century Barometer": a household appliance that supplies useful information from the internet on a daily basis. The projects were based on two sources of inspiration we looked at during the course:

We had an amazing visit from the entrepreneur Amar Latif. Amar, who is blind, mentioned how being able to tell what the weather is like outside is something he would find useful. Since these are closer to a traditional barometer, we decided to call them Amar's Barometers. They're marked with a blue background.

We also looked at research done by students from DJCAD's Master of Design Ethnography in partnership with NCR. They looked at how creative families in the cities of Shanghai, Paris and New York live, and how design can help improve their lives. They're marked with a brown background.
